Problem statement

In 2010, Kenya had 3.18Mha of natural forest, extending over 5.7% of its land area. In 2020, it lost 17.2kha of natural forest, equivalent to 7.68Mt of CO₂ of emissions.

Results

Explore interactive charts and maps that summarize key statistics about forests in Kenya. Statistics – including rates of forest change, forest extent, drivers of deforestation, and deforestation and fire alerts – can be customized, easily shared and downloaded for offline use.
